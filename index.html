From Roman church art to glass engineering

We turn sacred visuals into modern window systems.

The design language comes from Rome's churches: rose windows, fresco ceilings, mosaic icons, marble chapels, candlelight, and long shafts of colored glass. Joseph Stainedglass converts those references into custom stained glass, cut glass, and framed installations.

Basilica Arches

Become tall arched window systems for sanctuaries, foyers, stairwells, and dramatic feature walls.

Rose Window Geometry

Inspires circular, radial, and fan-pattern glazing that feels artistic without losing structure.

Fresco Color Stories

Guide warm amber, lapis blue, ruby, and ivory glass palettes for devotional interior light.

Mosaic Glass Panels

Translate church tilework into decorative, fluted, patterned, and privacy glass panels.
